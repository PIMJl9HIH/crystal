Using The Precision ControlTM
Advanced Technology Buttons
Water Button
For water spray, depress the left button only. Evolve will send a precise stream of water directly to the targeted area.
Air Button
To dispense air, depress the right button only. Evolve emits a powerful stream of air directly to the targeted area.
Both Buttons
By depressing both buttons simultaneously, you will get a powerful mist of atomized water and air in a wider spray pattern.
