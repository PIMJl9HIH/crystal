Evolve Disposable Air/Water Syringe Tip Material

Materials used in the Evolve disposable tip production are designed to be composted in aerobic industrial/municipal composting facilities where air, water, heat, and light break the product down. Using a compostable air/water syringe tip helps eliminate another stream of waste from entering our landfills. Dental staff can now feel pleased that they are complying with health standards endorsed worldwide, by using only single-use syringe tips, and be comfortable knowing that they are disposing of tips that are more environmentally safe than other leading brands.

Commercial/industrial composting facilities are growing steadily. We are at the forefront of a manufacturing transition. As the yearâ€™s progress and the environmental issues continue to grow, so will new facilities. Because the Evolve disposable air/water syringe tip is made from 100% renewable resources, even if disposed of in a conventional landfill, it will still be a more safe alternative for the environment than standard petroleum based plastics. On the other hand, many countries incinerate their rubbish. In this case, even though the materials are incinerated, because we have used only renewable resources, our tip will not deplete our finite fossil fuels.
