Refer A Friend.
Save Tomorrow.

With our Refer A Friend program, you can invite your colleagues to purchase Crystalgenics® Evolve. Each referral that orders you will receive a store credit of $150. You can then use this credit on any future order.

How Does It Work?
  • Register your Evolve account
  • When you sign into your account you will see Refer A Friend on the left side menu.
  • Click on Refer A Friend.
  • You will see your unique Evolve URL, example:
    Your Personalized Referral URL:
    https://www.crystalgenics.com/johnsmith2039488

    Send this URL to your referral. In order to receive credit for the order, your friend must access the Evolve website via clicking your URL. Click here to read more about Refer a Friend.

  • Copy and send that URL to your colleague.
Quick. Simple. And Easy.
How Do You Receive Credit For Your Referrals Order?

Once your colleague receives your URL. He or she MUST click on the URL you sent. If your colleague doesn’t click on URL and visits our website, you will not receive credit for order. Please make sure you tell your colleague to use your URL.

Once they click on your URL they will be directed to our website. They can then go ahead and place their Evolve order. Upon completing order, your account will be credited with $150 for any future order.

NOTE: The URL you send your clients allows the orders to be tracked. If they do not use your URL, there is no way to track the order, therefore you will not receive credit.
