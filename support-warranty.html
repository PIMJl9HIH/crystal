Warranty Terms & Conditions

General Characteristics

  • The Evolve air/water syringe is designed using state-of-the-art ergonomic concepts for easy use and immediate cleaning and sterilization.
  • Evolve air/water syringe is available in two profiles to fit individual preferences: Straight and Angled.
  • Evolve air/water syringe is available in 10 vibrant colors to help keep track of your sterilization order, or for personal preferences.
  • Evolve comes equipped with a factory-installed tip retainer system suitable for using ONLY the Evolve disposable air/water tip. As a single-use product, these tips further enhance your infection control protocols.
  • The shape of the syringe fits comfortably in any sized hand and the buttons are conveniently accessible to the thumb.
  • The syringe sleeve is easily removed from the body by depressing the One Touch Safety™.
  • Your Evolve air/water syringe should be autoclaved at 135˚C.
  • All products are CE certified.

Warranty

Syringe and tip retainer system carry a 3 year warranty from the date of the delivery document. Your Evolve air/water syringe must be registered within 30 days of receiving. If air/water syringe is not registered within the 30 day time period, warranty is null and void.

Any unauthorized manipulation or modification of the syringe and/or retainer system will immediately void the warranty and exonerate our company and its affiliates from all liability for injury or damage to equipment, persons or animals that may be caused by such modifications, manipulations, or improper use of Evolve.

Warranty covers the functional parts of the syringe: buttons, tip retainer, internal parts of the syringe, and excludes any damage to the color of the syringe sleeves, dents or scratches on sleeves and any labor and installation or removal costs. Warranty also excludes any wear and tear shown on the colors. Evolve uses a factory-installed disposable air/water syringe tip retainer to be used only with the Evolve disposable air/water syringe tips. Any other tip used in the Evolve system will automatically void any warranty. Placing syringe sleeve, syringe body, or tip retainer assembly into any cleaning solution, thermodisinfector, or ultrasonic cleaner will void warranty. Sleeve and retainer assembly should be sterilized ONLY in a steam autoclave.

All disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts of Orange County, CA, United States.

Installation And Connections

Installation and repairs of the air/water syringe may be performed ONLY by the dental unit manufacturer or its authorized technicians, or other technicians who have been trained and are qualified to install or perform repairs.

O-Ring maintenance on the tip retainer system may be performed by a qualified dentist, orthodontist, or hygienist, following the detailed maintenance instructions provided.

Normal Use

To dispense dental water into the operating field, press the left-hand button on the hand-piece.

To dispense dental air into the operating field, press the right-hand button on the hand-piece.

To dispense a more powerful spray of water with burst of air, press the two buttons on the hand-piece simultaneously.

Cleaning And Sterilization

After each use on a patient, the syringe sleeve must be cleaned and sterilized to guarantee maximum hygiene.

  • Remove the disposable air/water syringe tip from the retainer.
  • Press the one touch safety button at the bottom of the air/water syringe and slide the syringe sleeve off the body by pulling upwards, or outward.
  • Wipe clean and remove any stains before sterilizing.
  • Place in a steam autoclave at 135˚C for approximately 20 minutes.
  • DO NOT put in cleaning solutions, thermo-disinfector, or ultrasonic cleaner.

Maintenance

No specific maintenance of the air/water syringe is required, with the exception of normal cleaning and sterilization of syringe sleeves and tip retainer assemblies in a steam autoclave after each patient use.

O-Ring maintenance and replacement is recommended every six months to one year, depending on frequency of use. We use silicone O-Rings on the retainer system, which will hold up to autoclave sterilization and extend wear and tear time. To see O-Ring maintenance instructions, please click here.

Syringe and components should never be lubricated, or placed in an ultrasonic cleaner, cleaning solutions, or thermo-disinfector.

Disposal And Scrapping

The product does not contain any dangerous, toxic, or hazardous components. Nor does it come into contact with such products during the operating cycle. The tips are disposable and should be disposed of with any other dental materials using your normal disposal protocols. NEVER re-use the disposable air/water syringe tips.

Do’s And Dont’s

DO’S
  • Autoclave syringe sleeve after each patient use.
  • Leave tip retainer assembly on syringe when autoclaving.
  • Wipe down syringe sleeve with sanitary cloth or towel before and after each use.
  • Change O-Rings on the tip retainer assembly every 6 months to 1 year.
  • Insert syringe tip by lining up the twist-lock groove in tip retainer with groove on the Evolve tip. Push tip straight into tip retainer then twist clockwise to lock tip into place.
  • Remove syringe tip by twisting tip counter-clockwise to unlock tip from retainer. Then pull tip straight out of tip retainer.
DONT’S
  • Never sterilize the disposable air/water syringe tips.They are single-use ONLY.
  • Never re-use a disposable air/water syringe tips.
  • Never put syringe sleeves in ultrasonic cleaner, cleaning solutions, or thermo-disinfectors.
  • Never attempt to disassemble the Evolve syringe body. Only qualified technicians should ever perform syringe maintenance/repair.
  • Do not attempt to modify the Evolve air/water syringe. Any modifications or manipulations will void the warranty.
  • Do not lubricate Evolve syringe, sleeves, or components.
